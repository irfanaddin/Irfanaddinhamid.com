OUR SERVICES

Tax Preparation

The tax preparation process at Hamid Financials focuses on accuracy, efficiency, and timeliness. Whether you have a simple individual return or a complex business tax return, our goal is to ensure your return is prepared accurately, capturing every valuable deduction while abiding by the ever-changing rules of the IRS and other tax authorities.

  • Private Clients Services
  • U.S. Individual Tax Preparation
  • International Tax Preparation
    • U.S. Nonresident Alien Income Tax Return (1040-NR)
    • Dual-Status Returns
    • Passive Foreign Investment Company (PFIC) analysis and reporting
    • Voluntary Disclosure Programs
    • Delinquent FBAR reports
  • Business Services
    • U.S. Income Tax Return for a S Corporation (Form 1120S)
    • U.S. Return of Partnership Income (Form 1065)

  • Currently Not Collectible Status (CNC)
